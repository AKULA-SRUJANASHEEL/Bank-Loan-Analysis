# 🏦 Bank - Debit and Credit  Loan Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Bank -Debit and Credit - Loan Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution designed to evaluate lending performance, monitor portfolio health, assess credit risk, and provide actionable insights into customer borrowing behavior. The dashboard enables banking professionals and decision-makers to track key performance indicators (KPIs), identify trends, and support strategic lending decisions using data-driven analysis.

# 🎯 Project Objective

As a Data Analyst for a bank, the objective of this project is to analyze the bank's loan portfolio and generate actionable insights that improve lending decisions, reduce credit risk, and enhance portfolio performance.

### Business Objectives

* Monitor **Loan Application Trends**
* Evaluate **Loan Purpose Distribution**

# 🏛 Domain Knowledge

## Banking & Lending Process

Banks operate as financial intermediaries by accepting deposits from customers and lending those funds to individuals and businesses. Revenue is primarily generated through loan interest, processing fees, and investment income, while expenses include depositor interest, operational costs, and loan defaults.

### Revenue Sources

* Interest earned on loans
* Loan processing and origination fees
* Late payment penalties
* Investment income

### Operating Expenses

* Interest paid to depositors
* Loan defaults (Non-Performing Assets - NPAs)
* Operational expenses
* Borrowing costs

---

# 🔄 Loan Lifecycle

### 1. Loan Application

Customers submit loan applications by providing income, employment, loan purpose, and financial details.

### 2. Credit Assessment

Banks evaluate applicants based on:

* Creditworthiness
* Annual Income
* Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio
* Employment History
* Existing Credit Accounts

Loans are classified into **Grades (A–G)** and **Sub-Grades (A1–G5)** based on risk.

### 3. Loan Approval & Disbursement

Approved loans are sanctioned and disbursed.

### 4. Loan Repayment

Borrowers repay loans through monthly EMIs consisting of principal and interest.

### 5. Portfolio Monitoring

Banks continuously monitor repayment behavior, loan quality, defaults, and overall portfolio performance.
